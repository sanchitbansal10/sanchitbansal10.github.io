Quicknotes is a simple utility to jot down noes quickly and share it with others. 

## Features:
- Rich text editing
- Real-time saving
- Shareable notes
- No login required
- How to use:
- Start typing to create your note. Changes are saved automatically. Share the URL to let others view your note.

## Technologies Used

- Next.js
- MongoDB
- React
